About me

Hi, I'm Kari, a passionate knitter and crocheter with a love for all things handmade.

I started crocheting at the young age of 8, taught by my beloved grandmother, and I have been hooked ever since. In 2016, I discovered the world of knitting, and it quickly became my new obsession. I have since honed my skills in knitting and crochet, experimenting with different patterns and techniques to create beautiful and unique pieces.

In 2019, I started selling my knitting patterns on Etsy, sharing my love of knitting with others around the world. My patterns are carefully crafted with detailed instructions, designed to help even beginner knitters create beautiful and functional pieces.

I find joy and fulfillment in creating something with my own two hands, and I believe that everyone should experience the satisfaction of making something from scratch.

Thank you for visiting my website and supporting my small business. I hope my knitting patterns bring you as much joy and inspiration as they have brought me.

Happy knitting!
